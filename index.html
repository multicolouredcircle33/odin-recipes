Recipe Title Recipe for Applecider.

Applecider vinegar or cider vinegar, is a vinegar made from fermented apple juice and used in salad dressings,mandarines,vinaigerttes,food preservatives and chutneys.

The PH of apple cider vinegar is about 2-3,which is considerd mildly acidic.

Ingredients.

  • Apples.

Steps.

  1. 1. Place the apple pieces in the glass jar.
  2. 2. Fill the jar with filtered water.
  3. 3. Add in the sugar and shake until it dissolves.
  4. 4. Cover the jar with swatch of cloth and a rubber band.
  5. 5. Let the jar sit in a dark place for about 3-4 weeks.
  6. 6. Stir it occassionally and make sure that the apple pieces are fully submerged.
  7. 7. After 3-4 weeks strain out the apple pieces and compost.
  8. 8. Leave the liquid in the jar for another 3-4 weeks.
  9. 9. After then its ready for use.
